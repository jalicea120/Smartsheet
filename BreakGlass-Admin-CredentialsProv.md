## Secure Credential Provisioning Process

### Follow the steps below to create and securely share the Global Administrator account credentials with the client.
#### Step 1 — Access Keeper

1. Log into Aprio’s Keeper via Okta.
1. After successful authentication, open the Keeper Password Manager application.

<img width="155" height="160" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cbf77646-8a5a-455b-ac0e-007ad378a253" />

#### Step 2 — Create a New Record

1. Click **+ Create New** → select **Record Type: Login**  
1. Enter a clear Title for the record (e.g., “Break Glass Account – [Client Name]”)  
1. Under Login Information, enter the newly created admin email (e.g., breakglass-admin@tenant-name.onmicrosoft.us)  

#### Step 3 — Generate a Strong Password

1. Navigate to the Password field and paste the generated password by Microsoft.  
1. Click *Save* to store the record.  

#### Step 4 — Share Credentials Securely

1. Open the newly created Keeper record. 
1. In the top-right corner, click **Share → One-Time Share Link**  
1. Copy the generated link and send it to the client through a secure channel (e.g., encrypted email)
